Climber Strength Training Plan

This is a mobile-friendly 3-month strength training plan designed to support climbing performance and develop aesthetics in the chest, shoulders, lower abs, and obliques.

Pre-Workout Warm-up

  • 5 minutes light cardio (airbike, treadmill, or stairmaster)
  • Dynamic stretches: arm circles, hip openers, leg swings, shoulder rolls
  • 2 sets of scapular pull-ups (10 reps)
  • 1 light warm-up set of the first compound lift of the day

Post-Workout Cooldown

  • 3–5 minutes light walking or stationary cycling
  • Stretch: hamstrings, hip flexors, chest, lats, and shoulders (20–30 sec per hold)
  • Optional: Foam rolling quads, glutes, and upper back
  • Dry sauna (15 minutes) for relaxation and recovery
